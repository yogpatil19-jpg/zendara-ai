🚀 Step-by-Step Launch Guides

Follow these guides to publish your campaigns directly into Google Ads and Meta Ads Manager.

ℹ️ Before you start: Make sure you have a Google account and your campaign generated in Zendara AI. You'll need your credit card for billing setup in Google Ads.
1
Go to Google Ads and create an account
Visit ads.google.com and click "Start now". Sign in with your Google account. When asked "What's your main advertising goal?" — select "Get more calls" or "Get more website sales or sign-ups". Click "Switch to Expert Mode" at the bottom to access full campaign controls.
↗ Open Google Ads
2
Create a new Search campaign
Click "+ New campaign" → Select goal: "Leads" → Campaign type: "Search" → Click Continue. Enter your campaign name from Zendara (copy from the Campaign Overview section).
3
Set bidding strategy and budget
Bidding: Select "Maximise conversions" (recommended for new campaigns) or the strategy Zendara specified. Budget: Enter the Daily Budget from Zendara's Campaign Overview. Set start date to today and no end date.
4
Set location and language targeting
Location: Click "Enter another location" → Type your city (e.g. "Auckland") → Select it → Click "Nearby" to set radius. Language: Select English. Set location options to "Presence: People in or regularly in your targeted locations".
5
Create your Ad Group and add keywords
Name your ad group (use the Ad Group names from Zendara). In the keyword box, paste each keyword from Zendara's keyword list. Format: Exact Match uses [brackets], Phrase Match uses "quotes", Broad Match is plain text.
6
Create your Responsive Search Ad
Click "+ New ad". Enter your website URL. Copy and paste each headline from Zendara into the 15 headline fields — the character counter will confirm they fit. Paste all 4 descriptions. Click "Save and continue".
7
Add negative keywords
Go to Keywords → Negative keywords. Click "+" → Add to campaign level. Paste the negative keywords list from Zendara one per line. These prevent irrelevant clicks that waste your budget.
8
Add ad extensions (assets)
Go to Ads & Assets → Assets. Add Sitelinks (4), Callouts (6), and Call extension from Zendara. These significantly improve click-through rate and are free to add.
9
Publish and verify
Click "Publish campaign". Google will review your ads (usually 1–3 hours, sometimes up to 24h for professional services). You'll get an email when approved. Check the Campaign Status shows "Eligible" ✓
📌 Need your campaign content? Generate it first in the Google Ads AI tab.
ℹ️ Before you start: You need a Facebook Business Page and a Meta Business Suite account. Visit business.facebook.com to set this up (free, takes ~10 minutes).
1
Open Meta Ads Manager
Go to adsmanager.facebook.com. Click "+ Create". Select your campaign objective — use "Leads" for professional services (this enables the native Lead Form).
↗ Open Meta Ads Manager
2
Name your campaign and set budget
Use the Campaign Name from Zendara. Set Campaign Budget Optimisation (CBO) to ON. Enter the Monthly Budget from Zendara. Select Advantage+ placements for best results.
3
Set up Ad Set 1 — Cold Audience
Name: Use Ad Set 1 name from Zendara. Location: Enter your city, set radius as specified. Age: Enter age range. Detailed Targeting: Click "Browse" and add each interest from Zendara's Detailed Targeting list. Set Exclusions from Zendara's excluded audiences list.
4
Create your ads (3 variations)
For each ad variation from Zendara: Click "+ Add creative". Upload or create your image (use the Image Brief for guidance — Canva is free). Paste Primary Text, Headline, Description. Select the CTA button. Repeat for all 3 variations.
↗ Create your ad image in Canva (free)
5
Set up the Lead Form
If using Lead Generation objective: Click "Create form". Enter the Headline and Description from Zendara's Lead Form section. Add the 3 questions from Zendara. Enter the Thank You message. Click "Save".
6
Set up Ad Set 2 — Retargeting
Duplicate your first Ad Set. Rename it to "Retargeting". Change the audience to: Custom Audience → Website visitors (requires Facebook Pixel installed on your website). Use the Retargeting message angle from Zendara — adjust your ad copy for warm audiences.
7
Install Facebook Pixel on your website
Go to Events Manager → Pixels → Add. Copy your Pixel ID. Install it on your website (via Google Tag Manager, WordPress plugin, or paste in site header). This enables retargeting and conversion tracking.
↗ Open Events Manager
8
Publish and monitor
Click "Publish". Meta reviews ads for professional services within 24h. Check the Delivery column shows "Active". In the first 3–5 days, don't make changes — let Meta's algorithm learn your audience.
📌 Need your Meta campaign content? Generate it first in the Meta Ads AI tab.
